Cyber Threat Intelligence

Learn how to protect mobile devices and applications from evolving cyber threats.

Service Details

This training program focuses on the processes and techniques used to gather, analyze, and act on cyber threat intelligence. Participants will learn about the different types of threat intelligence, sources of intelligence data, and the use of threat intelligence platforms. The course covers topics such as threat actor profiling, attack vector analysis, and threat intelligence sharing. Hands-on exercises will provide practical experience in collecting and analyzing threat data, developing intelligence reports, and using threat intelligence to inform security decisions. By the end of the course, participants will be able to leverage threat intelligence to proactively defend against cyber threats.
